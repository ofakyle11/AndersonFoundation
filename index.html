How we help

Cancer costs more than treatment. We stand with patients and survivors where the system leaves gaps.

Financial assistance

Help with medical and dental bills, transportation to treatment, and the everyday costs that pile up when treatment interrupts work and life.

Mental-health support

Counselling, support groups, and honest conversation for patients, survivors, and families — because the fight doesn't end when treatment does.

Community & events

From trivia nights to the Tree of Life run at Tough Mudder, we bring people together to raise funds and remind each other we're not alone.

Angus's story

In September 2019, Angus Anderson's cancer journey began with severe injuries to his jaw — broken bone fragments and the loss of six teeth. What followed was a fight not just against cancer, twice, but against everything that comes with it: lost work, mounting out-of-pocket bills, and the toll all of it takes on your mind.

He came through it determined that the next person wouldn't have to face those battles alone. That determination became this foundation.
