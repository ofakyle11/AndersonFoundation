Your gift changes someone's fight

Cancer comes with bills, missed paycheques, and heavy days. Your donation covers the gaps — dental and medical costs, rides to treatment, and mental-health support.

Ways to give

Give online

The fastest way to help — donate securely through our fundraising page.

Interac e-transfer

Send an e-transfer to angusandersonfoundation@gmail.com and write "donation" in the message. Add a runner's name to support them in Tough Mudder.

Sponsor a runner

Back a member of Team Tough Mudder Toronto and watch their total climb on the live dashboard.

Meet the runners

Where the money goes

  • Medical and dental bills not covered by insurance
  • Transportation to and from treatment centres
  • Counselling and mental-health programs
  • Emergency support when treatment interrupts work

Corporate & in-kind giving

Businesses can sponsor an event, match employee donations, or contribute goods and services — from venue space to care-package supplies. Email info@angusandersonfoundation.org and we'll find the right fit.
