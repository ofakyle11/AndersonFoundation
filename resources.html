Resources

If you're struggling right now, you don't have to figure it out alone. Start here.

If you need help now

Free, confidential support across Canada — day or night.

  • 9-8-8 Suicide Crisis Helpline Call or text 9-8-8

    24/7 support in English and French for anyone thinking about suicide or worried about someone.

  • Wellness Together Canada 1-866-585-0445

    Free mental-health and substance-use support, from self-guided tools to live counselling.

  • Canadian Cancer Society Support Line 1-888-939-3333

    Information about cancer, treatment, and community supports — for patients, survivors, and caregivers.

  • Wellspring wellspring.ca

    Free programs — counselling, peer support, money matters — for anyone living with cancer, no referral needed.

Requesting help from the foundation

If cancer has left you or someone you love struggling with bills, transportation to treatment, or access to mental-health support, reach out. Tell us a little about your situation at info@angusandersonfoundation.org — every request is read by a real person and kept confidential.

A formal application process (eligibility and forms) is being finalized by the board and will be posted here.

Refund policy

Donations are gratefully received and put to work quickly. If you made a donation in error — a duplicate payment or an incorrect amount — contact us at info@angusandersonfoundation.org within 30 days and we'll make it right.

Event registration fees may be refundable up to 14 days before the event date; contact us and we'll do our best to accommodate.
